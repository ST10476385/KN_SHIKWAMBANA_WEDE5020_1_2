Volunteer Information

Volunteering with Baked by Ruth is a rewarding way to give back to the community. Volunteers help with event setup, serving, cleanup, and more. No experience needed—just a willing heart and a smile!

  • Flexible shifts for all events
  • Meet new friends and make a difference
  • Community service certificates available
  • Snacks and refreshments provided

More Details

Contact Lead: Nomsa Dlamini — +27 123 456 789 | volunteer@bakedbyruth.co.za

Typical Shift: 3–5 hours. Morning (08:00–12:00) and afternoon (13:00–17:00) slots available.

What to Bring: Comfortable, closed-toe shoes, a mask if preferred, and a positive attitude. We provide gloves and aprons as needed.

Accessibility: If you need any accommodations (mobility, hearing, or other), please contact us and we'll do our best to support you.

How it works: After you sign up we'll email you a brief orientation and your assigned shift. If you can't make your shift, please let us know in advance so we can reassign.
